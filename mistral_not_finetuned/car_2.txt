Lego Pieces Needed:
4x Lego Classic Brick 1x2 (Part Number: 3004)
2x Lego Classic Plate 1x2 (Part Number: 3023)
2x Lego Classic Brick 1x1 (Part Number: 3005)
2x Lego Classic Plate 1x1 (Part Number: 3022)
2x Lego Classic Tile 1x2 (Part Number: 3068)
2x Lego Classic Tile 1x1 (Part Number: 3070)
2x Lego Classic Brick 1x2 Corner (Part Number: 11211)
1x Lego Classic Plate 2x2 (Part Number: 3021)
1x Lego Classic Brick 2x2 (Part Number: 3003)
1x Lego Classic Angular Plate 1x2 (Part Number: 3676)
1x Lego Classic Slope 30 Degrees 2x1 (Part Number: 3037)
1x Lego Classic Wheel 2x2 (Part Number: 627)
1x Lego Classic Axle 3L (Part Number: 6563)
1x Lego Classic Axle Pin with Friction Ridges Lengthwise (Part Number: 33440)
Story:
Get ready to hit the road in style with this sleek and sporty Lego small car! Featuring a modern design, smooth curves, and shiny wheels, this car is perfect for cruising around town or racing on the open road.
Step-by-step Guide:
Place two 1x2 bricks (3004) on a 2x2 plate (3021), side by side.
Attach two 1x1 plates (3022) on top of the bricks, centered.
Add two 1x1 bricks (3005) on the front and back of the 1x1 plates.
Connect two 1x2 corner plates (11211) to the front and back of the 1x1 bricks.
Place two 1x2 tiles (3068) on the corner plates, covering the 1x1 bricks.
Attach two 1x1 tiles (3070) on the top corners of the 1x2 tiles.
Add a 1x2 plate (3023) in front of the base, centered.
Place a 30-degree slope (3037) on the left and right sides of the 1x2 plate.
Attach a wheel (627) to each of the axle pins (33440) using the axle (6563).
Insert the axle pins with the attached wheels into the holes on the bottom of the base.
Connect the angular plate (3676) to the front of the base, centered.
Attach a 1x2 brick (3004) on top of the angular plate, centered.
Place a 1x2 tile (3068) on top of the 1x2 brick, centered.
Attach a 1x1 tile (3070) on top of the 1x2 tile, centered.
Place a 1x1 plate (3022) on top of the 1x1 tile, centered.
Attach a 1x1 brick (3005) on top of the 1x1 plate, centered.
Finish the build by placing a 1x1 tile (3070) on top of the 1x1 brick, centered.
Congratulations, you have completed your Lego small car! Get ready to hit the road and show off your stylish new ride!
Optional: Add a second set of wheels to the back of the car for a more complete look.
To do this, insert another axle pin with friction ridges (33440) into the hole on the back of the base, then attach a wheel (627) to each end of the axle pin.
